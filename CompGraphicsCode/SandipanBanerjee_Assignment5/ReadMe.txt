Steps to run Assignment5.py:-

1. Open cmd (command prompt) script
2. Go to the directory location (cd .../SandipanBanerjee_Assignment5)
3. Type 'python Assignment5.py' and press Enter
4. The car model should appear on the screen with texture 
5. There should be a point light (white) and a spot light (green) with a 5 degree circle on the car
6. The back wall should have an animation with the image texture zooming in and out 
7. Press 'i' or 'I' to zoom in to the car
8. Press 'o' or 'O' to zoom out of the car
9. Press 's' or 'S' to turn the spot light source on/off
10. Press 'p' or 'P' to turn the point light source on/off
11. Press 'f' or 'F' to save current frame as a PPM image
12. Click and hold the left click button on the mouse for rotating the car in an anti-clockwise direction
13. Press 'q' or 'Q' to quit and close window
